About Elevate UIC

Working together to create a stronger, more inclusive university experience.

Elevate UIC are Undergraduate Student Government (USG) leaders running to continue elevating the student experience by strengthening UIC’s community and advocating for the needs of you, the student. Led by current USG Student Success Director Nathan, USG VP Dilay, and UIC Student Trustee Quinn, Elevate UIC is committed to pushing the momentum forward in advocating for lowering the cost of our education, addressing food insecurity on campus, and fighting for student rights.

Our Core Pillars

Protecting Student Safety & Wellness

From campus safety to mental health resources, we ensure that every student's fundamental needs are met.

Forging Student Success

We focus on expanding academic support, professional development, and inclusive advising services.

Strengthen Our UIC Community

We strive to bolster student organizations, encourage athletic attendance, and foster campus pride.

Elevating Campus Life

Through spirited events, stronger student org support, and revitalized spaces, we’re building a more vibrant and connected UIC community.

Our Platform

Our comprehensive plan to improve student life at UIC.

Reducing Your Costs

Student-focused review on reducing the cost of higher education at UIC

  • Comprehensive task force: Establish a task force to review UIC’s financial practices and ensure accountability in tuition and fee use.
  • Better advising: Improve our financial aid/loan advising to work for all students.
  • State support: Advocate for increased state financial support for UIC students.
  • Free textbooks: Champion open educational resources (OER) to expand access to free or low-cost textbooks.
  • CTA & Metra U-Pass+: Work toward permanently establishing the U-Pass+ at UIC, reducing commuting costs and improving transit accessibility.
  • UIC student discounts: Launch a centralized discount guide, connecting students with deals across the city.

Protecting Student Safety & Wellness

Creating a secure and healthy campus environment for every student

  • Review & improve safety: Utilize the Campus Safety Commission to enact change with admin and UICPD.
  • Expand Night Ride: Push for extended Night Ride service times and coverage for late-night transit.
  • Enhance campus lighting: Upgrade and expand lighting across walkways, parking lots, and common areas.
  • Mental health services: Continue improving service capacity with tailored solutions for all students.
  • Healthy food options: Provide cost and dietary-friendly food options on and around campus.
  • Digitize your I-Card: Advocate for digital mobile I-Card and secure swipe access for buildings.

Forging Student Success

Ensuring every student has the resources to excel academically and professionally

  • Interview meeting rooms: Provide quiet, private booths for virtual meetings.
  • Improve campus Wi-Fi: Advocate for stronger, more reliable connectivity in all buildings.
  • Professional development: Offer skill-building workshops, networking events, free headshots, and resume reviews.
  • Enhanced advising & tutoring: Expand resources to ensure exceptional academic and career guidance for every major.

Elevating Campus Life

Building a vibrant, engaged, and spirited community at UIC

  • Support & fund student orgs: Streamline SAFC funding processes and support campus events.
  • Develop UIC’s spirit: Promote athletics with tailgates, giveaways, and athlete-student interactions.
  • Frequent free events: Organize free food days, bowling nights, and activities to unite the community.
  • Activate our Chicago network: Expand partnerships with local firms, events, and alumni to enrich campus life.
  • Improve public spaces: Transform underutilized areas into lounges and social spaces for students.
